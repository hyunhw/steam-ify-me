About Steam-ify your DLC


This application accepts a single user's Steam ID and predicts whether or not the individual would enjoy the selected game's expansion given the expansion's (DLC) info such as price and release date. Play around with it and have fun! videogame_asset

This product uses the Steam API to retrieve games owned by the user in real-time and uses a pre-trained Random Forest algorithm to make the prediction. More details on the data-pipeline is shown in the slides below.


This product was built as a part of my Insight Data Science program.
